Rangotsav 2018

Rangoli Competition

Rangotsav 2019,orgainsed by Navprayas team, witnessed a huge increment in participations this year. We work around different themes and ideas to spread social messages and progess towards social upliftment.
The themes were Hariyali(Greenery), Sab padhe Sab badhe (Education for all), Pradushan(Pollution), Khel(sports). Their hardwork, creativity and dedication altogether spread messages of societal construction.

Career Counselling

8 June 2018

To build career in one's life, one needs the right guidance to acheive success. So, This time Navprayas Team organised career counselling for class 10th students and 10th passed students to provide right guidance to help students to choose their right career path in their life. Career paths in different sector like Engineering, Medical, Arts, Commerce etc. were explained to the students so that they can choose their path wisely. Thanks to students for their great response and also special thanks to Ravi Ranjan, Aashish Kumar, Amandeep Kumar, Rupa Kumari and all other speakers who guided the students.

Our flagship event

With a time limit for 3 hours, some students showed their academic excellence by solving more questions efficiently.
